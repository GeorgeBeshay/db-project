Our team at the University of Alexandria's Computer and Systems Engineering department is dedicated to creating impactful solutions that transcend mere technology. We amalgamate our diverse skill sets, passion for innovation, and commitment to societal change to craft systems like the Pet Adoption and Shelter Management System.

Microsoft SQL Server, our chosen database management system, forms the backbone of our platform, ensuring a robust, secure, and scalable infrastructure. Our software engineering process encapsulated comprehensive planning, agile development methodologies, and stringent quality assurance to yield a user-centric application that streamlines pet adoption.

By blending our technical prowess with an acute understanding of the animal welfare landscape, we have endeavored to create a platform that empowers shelters, administrators, and adopters alike. Our constant pursuit of excellence propels us to refine and enhance our system continually.

As a team, we are unwavering in our belief that technology can serve a higher purpose, fostering compassion and care for our furry companions. With a focus on innovation and empathy, we aim to make a meaningful difference in the lives of both animals and the individuals dedicated to their well-being.
